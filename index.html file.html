Portfolio

HCI stands for Human-Computer Interaction. It's a multidisciplinary field that focuses on the design, evaluation, and implementation of interactive computing systems for human use. HCI involves studying how people interact with technology and aims to create user-friendly and efficient interfaces for various devices and applications, such as software, websites, and hardware. It combines elements of computer science, psychology, design, and usability to ensure that technology is user-centered and intuitive.
